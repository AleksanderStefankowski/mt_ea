2. OnDeinit() — runs when EA stops.
3. OnTick() — runs on every tick.

5. OnTrade() — simplified trade event.
6. OnTimer() — scheduled execution.
7. EventSetTimer() — enable timer events.
8. EventKillTimer() — disable timer.
